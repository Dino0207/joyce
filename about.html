About Us

 SIT & STAY is your neighborhood spot for exceptional coffee and a relaxing atmosphere. Founded on the principles of quality, comfort, and community, we aim to be the perfect place for you to unwind, work, or connect with friends. We carefully source our beans and craft each drink with passion, ensuring a delightful experience with every visit. Come discover why we're the ideal place to sit, stay, and savor.

So, whether you're a regular or visiting for the first time, we invite you to pull up a chair, relax, and become a part of our story.

Join us!! We're thrilled to share our passion for coffee and community with you.
